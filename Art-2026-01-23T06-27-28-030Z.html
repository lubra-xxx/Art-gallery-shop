Peaceful Slumber

This exquisite piece is a close-up of a sleeping cockatoo showcasing its soft feathers alongside spring green foliage brushed with specks of light, inviting a heart-warming sense of peace and serenity. Every detail- from the glossy eyes to the moisture on its feathers- is captured impeccably on 170 gsm (65 lb) semi-glossy print. It's mounted on a durable black pine wood frame, preserving its minimalist appeal with ample negative space and an alluring focus on no more than two subjects. An 8k ultra high-definition masterpiece, the artist's intention with this image was to infuse peace amidst chaos, evoking a sense of warmth with every glance at this fine work.
